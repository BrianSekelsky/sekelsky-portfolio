I am a Master of Graphic & Experience Design candidate at North Carolina State University. My focus is on interaction design, web, 3D, and user experience. I am currently working as a researcher on a collaborative project between educators, designers, and engineers. Together, we are creating Scale Worlds as part of a grant from the National Science Foundation.

Previously, I obtained a BS in Computer Science from the University of Wisconsin–Madison, worked as a web developer, and spent 27 months teaching in Tanzania as a Peace Corps Volunteer. In my free time I enjoy distance running, recording electronic and folk music, and exploring the great outdoors.

I am currently looking for a job to start upon graduation May 2023.
