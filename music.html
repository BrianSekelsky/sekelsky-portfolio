My Music

Outside of my work as a designer, I maintain a creative practice in electronic/folk music production and internet art.

Winter Foe

I started my project called Winter Foe in 2018. Through this project I have learned a ton about visual design and had the opportunity to collaborate with some amazingly creative people. Mostly instrumental songs which incoporate elements of electronic and folk music. I also have produced music under this alias for and with a number of talented musicians including rosarrie, park bird, and Fjun.

I will find time to write more about this project soon.
